# Story Decisions

Story decisions should survive beyond the conversation that produced them. Record the choice, the alternatives, and the reason.

## What To Record

- Canon choices: facts about characters, world, timeline, powers, relationships.
- Direction choices: selected plot path, scene purpose, emotional target, POV.
- Rejected alternatives: ideas the author considered and declined.
- Constraints: what future writing must preserve.
- Open questions: decisions intentionally left unresolved.

## Where To Put Decisions

Keep decisions close to the artifact they affect:

- character decisions in `kb/characters/`;
- worldbuilding decisions in `kb/world/`;
- chronology decisions in `kb/timeline/`;
- style decisions in `kb/styles/`;
- draft-specific decisions in `work/` until they become durable.

## Format

Use short source-aware notes:

```markdown
<!-- decision: The protagonist hides the jade token until chapter 12.
reason: The reveal needs to reframe the sect trial rather than solve the first conflict.
rejected: Revealing it in chapter 3 made the early arc too easy.
source: planning notes, 2026-05-02. -->
```

If the user has not confirmed a choice, label it as tentative instead of canon.
